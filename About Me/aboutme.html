About Me

Hi! My name is Bri Christiansen and I am a Web Design and Development Major at BYU-Idaho. This is my second to last semester and I am super excited to graduate!!!! In my time as a Web Design and Development Major, I have taken many classes to learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, node.js, python, and MySQL. I have also taken design classes using the Adobe products, and a social media marketing class which was one of my favorites. When I graduate I am excited to stay at my current job as a web designer for Handy Optimal, and continue making epic websites for various clients in Idaho, Arizona, and Utah. Aside from web design and development, I am also in the Jazz choir on campus called Vocal Union. At the end of this semester we will be going on tour to Florida and I am super excited about that. I have earned a talent scholarship as part of being in the group, as well as an academic scholarship for my grades. Outside of school, I have been married to my husband for 2 years in April, and we have a super cute Husky named Stormy.

Projects

Rexburg Car Shop

Me and the rest of my team at Handy Optimal helped design and implement this website for a car mechanic shop local to Rexburg Idaho. I found a layout using Wordpress with the divi theme. I then replaced, changed, and added my own code to change the layout from what it was to what it looks like now. We kept a few features from the layout but it was really just a starting point to get us to where it is now.

The Diamond P Ranch

I started this project at the beginning of January and we will be going live at the end of the month. This is a website for a horseback riding company located near West Yellowtone. I have really enjoyed making this website and it has been such a neat opportunity for me. It was one of my first solo projects at my job.
